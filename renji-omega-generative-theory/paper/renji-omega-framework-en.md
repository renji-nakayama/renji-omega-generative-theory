# **Abstract**

This paper introduces **Generative Theory**, a unified framework that explains how worlds, structures, and selves emerge from first principles. The theory begins with the **Renji‑Ω field**, an undifferentiated, fully symmetric substrate that contains maximal generative potential. Minimal symmetry breaking yields the **four generative directions**—Dir, Ent, Was, and His—which form the irreducible basis for coherence, spreading, geometry, and temporal consistency. Generative Theory provides a minimal and complete account of how world, structure, and self arise as a single continuous generative flow.
